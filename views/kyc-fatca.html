The Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA) is a United States Federal Law that requires the US tax residents including those living abroad to report their financial accounts held offshore, and requires foreign financial institutions to report to the Internal Revenue Services (IRS) about their U.S. clients. Indian Govt has signed the treaty with US and consequently all Indian Mutual Fund Investors are required to provide this information.
